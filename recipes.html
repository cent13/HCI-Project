Banana & Almond Butter Smoothie Bowl

Servings: 2
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Ingredients:
2 frozen bananas
1 cup unsweetened almond milk
2 tbsp almond butter
1 tbsp chia seeds
1 tbsp maple syrup (optional)
1 cup mixed berries (for topping)
1/4 cup granola (for topping)
Instructions:
Blend: Combine the frozen bananas, almond milk, almond butter, chia seeds, and maple syrup in a blender. Blend until smooth and creamy. Serve: Pour the smoothie into bowls.
Top: Add mixed berries and granola on top. Enjoy immediately.

Savory Chickpea Omelette

Servings: 2
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Ingredients:
1 cup chickpea flour
1/2 tsp turmeric
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black salt (Kala Namak, for eggy flavor, optional)
1 cup water
1 cup chopped vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, onions, spinach)
1 tbsp olive oil
Instructions:
Prepare Batter: In a bowl, whisk together the chickpea flour, turmeric, garlic powder, salt, black salt, and water until smooth. Cook Veggies: Heat olive oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add chopped vegetables and saut√© for 3-4 minutes until softened. Cook Omelette: Pour the chickpea batter into the skillet over the vegetables. Cook for 3-4 minutes on one side until bubbles form on the surface and the edges start to lift. Flip and cook the other side for another 3 minutes. Serve: Cut into wedges and serve warm.
